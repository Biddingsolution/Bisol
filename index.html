⚡ What is Electrical Contractor Registration?

The Electrical Contractor Licence is issued by the Delhi Electrical Inspectorate, GNCT of Delhi under the Indian Electricity Act. It is mandatory for any entity that undertakes electrical installation, maintenance, or repair work in Delhi — whether for government tenders or private projects.

Without this licence, you cannot legally bid for any electrical work tender, and ongoing projects can be stopped by the inspectorate. Our team handles the complete application, documentation, and follow-up process.
