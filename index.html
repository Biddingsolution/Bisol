Tender Filing & Bidding

End-to-End Tender Assistance

From identifying the right tenders to submitting a winning bid — our team handles every step so you can focus on delivery.

Our Process
1
Eligibility Assessment
We review your company's turnover, experience, certifications, and registration status to identify which tenders you qualify for right now — and what's needed to qualify for more.
2
Tender Identification & Shortlisting
Our team monitors GeM, CPPP, eProcure, and state portals daily. We shortlist tenders that match your profile and alert you before deadlines.
3
Document Preparation
We prepare all required documents — technical bids, financial bids, experience certificates, undertakings, EMD arrangements — checked for compliance against NIT conditions.
4
Portal Filing & Submission
Our team handles the actual portal submission with digital signatures, ensuring every document is correctly uploaded and no mandatory field is missed.
5
Post-Bid Support
We track L1 results, handle clarification queries, assist with performance bank guarantees, and support work order acceptance when you win.

Key Benefits of DPIIT Recognition
💰
EMD Exemption on All Central Tenders
Save lakhs per year that would otherwise be locked in as Earnest Money Deposits across multiple tender applications.

Prior Experience Relaxation
New startups can apply for tenders even without prior government contract experience — a major barrier removed.

Turnover Criterion Relaxation
Minimum turnover criteria is relaxed for DPIIT startups, allowing early-stage companies to compete for larger contracts.
